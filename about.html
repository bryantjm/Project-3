About Me

I used to go to IU Bloomington where I was in the Informatics program. After a year or so I switched to Ivy Tech where I took a few semesters in Culinary Arts and Hospitality Management. I'm currently a senior at IUPUI in General Studies: Science and math Concentration with a focus on Computer Science and Psychology.

I suppose one of the things I'd like to accomplish with this kind of knowledge is to design new user interfaces for different computer systems and applications(since so many out there seem to infuriate me). Besides simply the UI layout, I'd also like to work with implementing things such as motion detection, user feedback(like sounds and vibration),"touchable" holograms, and so on. I feel that providing a more natural means of manipulating data and media can greatly increase our ability to create and share information. I also like to make a few basic video games here and there(mostly just with Blender).
